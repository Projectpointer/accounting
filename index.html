Frequently Asked Questions
How does your payroll handle tax deductions?
Our payroll engine automatically calculates federal, state, and local taxes based on employee W-4 information. We also generate tax payment reports and file forms (941, W-2) on your behalf.
Can I integrate with my existing accounting software?
Absolutely. LedgerFlow supports native integration with QuickBooks, Xero, and REST APIs. You can sync invoices, expenses, and journal entries in real time.
Is there a free trial for payroll & accounting?
Yes, we offer a 30-day free trial with full access to accounting and payroll features. No credit card required, and onboarding support included.
How does multi-entity accounting work?
Our platform supports unlimited entities under one subscription. You can consolidate reports, manage inter-company transactions, and run payroll per entity.
