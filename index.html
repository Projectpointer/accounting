Our engine automatically calculates federal, state, and local taxes from employee W-4 data. We generate 941, W-2, and state filings, and remit payments on your behalf.

Yes. LedgerFlow syncs seamlessly with QuickBooks Online, Xero, and offers a REST API for custom integrations.
